Chili's Tips for Kids - Wednesday, November 15th, 2017

Join us Wednesday, November 15th from 5-9p.m. for good food and fun at Chili's for the Annual Cops for Kids Christmas Fundraiser. It's the perfect opportunity to help the less fortunate children in our community. Your generous donations are greatly appreciated and will help these children in their time of need. Law enforcement officers from Lake Elsinore station will be your hosts and waiters for the evening. We look forward to seeing you there!


Unity in the Community - April 21, 2018

Our 2018 and 17th edition race will be held on Saturday, April 21st at the Lake Elsinore Levee with start time at 7:45 This year we will have finishers medals for everyone in both the 5k and 10k!! We will have professional chip timing once again.* Must pre-register to guarantee. And since its such a great cause, we are teaming up with Cops for Kids again! We encourage everyone to bring a new unwrapped toy for their program the day of the race. We will have the same great school incentives from last year so be sure to get your school involved to win great prizes! We are still on of the lowest cost races in the area.
